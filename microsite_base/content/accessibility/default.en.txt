## Accessibility & Inclusive Use

{{ companyName }} aims to provide a website that everyone can use comfortably, regardless of ability or limitation. Our focus is on offering an online space that is clear, practical, and usable for the widest audience possible.  

## Our Approach
At {{ companyName }}, we work toward accessibility by:  
- Designing our site to function with assistive technologies.  
- Adding descriptive alternatives for images and media.  
- Using straightforward layouts and language for clarity.  
- Providing captions or transcripts for audio and video content.  
- Carrying out regular checks to improve usability.  

## Key Features
We’ve built in tools to improve access, such as:  
- **Adjustable text sizes** so visitors can read comfortably.  
- **Keyboard-only navigation** for those who don’t use a mouse.  
- **High-contrast visuals** to assist with low vision.  
- **Screen reader support** for users with vision impairments.  

## Contact
If you encounter any difficulty or have suggestions, contact us at {{ email }}.  
Your feedback helps us keep {{ companyName }} accessible to all.
